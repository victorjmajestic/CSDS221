Victor's Minesweeper
This is a 10 by 10 grid with 25 bombs.
Left click on a square to see what is underneath.
If you reveal a bomb, GAME OVER!
If you reveal a number, the square is touching that number of bombs.
Right click a square you suspect a bomb is under to place a flag on the square.
Right click on a flagged square to remove the flag.
Place a flag on all 25 correct squares and you WIN!
Refresh the page to start over or try again.
